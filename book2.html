    Editors Review

    A friend working at Google recommended this book when I was researching better performance management. This book transforms my mindset on leadership and culture in an organisation. It encourages readers to lead the operations of an organisation with creativity, empathy and data. Inspired by the book, I ran an innovation programme within my organisation, a 5000 people global consultancy, with the principles I learnt. It was a huge success, including a 10-fold increase in staff engagement. Strongly recommend the book to anyone who is passionate about people.
