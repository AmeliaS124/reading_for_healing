Pet Lover

    • Book Blurb

      Charles and Lizzie Peterson love puppies. They want a puppy of their own more than just about anything. They know dogs are a lot of work. But their mom still doesn't think their family is ready for a puppy. Then, Goldie arrives. She is a sweet golden retriever who needs a home. Goldie is very young. She doesn't know how to be a good puppy yet. Will Charles and Lizzie be able to help her?

      Ellen Miles
    Editors Review

    Goldie is the very first book in the Puppy Place series. It is about two children and their family who decide to foster a golden retriever puppy. This series is a great beginner level (Grades 2-4) book for the whole family to enjoy. It teaches children about the true expectations of having a puppy and when house training, lead training and socializing gets a bit much you can always put the book down! For me it brings great nostalgia to think about and I remember many of the lessons taught throughout this series today.
