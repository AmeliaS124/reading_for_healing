    • Men Are from Mars, Women Are from Venus: The Classic Guide to Understanding the Opposite Sex

      In his classic guide to understanding the opposite sex, Dr John Gray, provides a practical and proven way for people to improve their communication and relationships by acknowledging the differences between them. The book helps people realise how different they can be in their communication styles, their emotional needs, and their modes of behaviour, and offers the secrets of communicating without conflicts.

      John Gray 1992

    Editors Review

    Before reading the book, I was sceptical about its relevance to the modern world as the book was first published 28 years ago (1992). I was then amazed by the examples given and some step-by-step guides for readers to practise during interactions. The book was originally designed for couples to improve their communication and relationships. However, it also forms a very good basis to practise 'empathy', a popular topic in diversity and inclusion. Whilst the landscape of technologies is changing rapidly in the last decade, the way of how people feel remains. I have no reservation to recommend this timeless book to anyone who would like to enjoy the power bought by good communications with empathy.

    Monique Ho 5 March 2019
