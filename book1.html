    • Editors Review

      The book has the power of transforming mindset and makes readers feel ‘heard’. With a great sense of humour, Meng explained meaningful topics such as mental health, happiness and creativity thoroughly in simple words. The book empowers very deep reflection on my core values. It gives lots of answers, untangling some knots and unresolved emotions so I can ‘find myself’ again. Many step-by-step guides to help you practise the newly introduced concepts. Highly recommend those who would like to improve livelihood and productivity.

      Monique Ho
